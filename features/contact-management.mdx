Trellixe helps you build and maintain meaningful professional relationships by providing a centralized contact management system. Store contact information, track how you met each person, and maintain detailed interaction histories.

## Adding a New Contact

You can add contacts to your account from the Contacts page:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Contacts">
    Click on "Contacts" in your main navigation menu. You'll see the total count of contacts in your account displayed in the page header.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open the Contact Form">
    Click the user icon button in the top right corner (desktop) or the floating blue button in the bottom right (mobile).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Contact Details">
    Fill out the contact form with the following information:
    
    - **First Name** (required) - The contact's given name
    - **Last Name** - The contact's family name
    - **Email** - Email address for the contact
    - **Phone Number** - Contact phone number
    - **How We Met** - A text area to record context about your relationship
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save the Contact">
    Click "Create Contact" to add them to your account. The contact will be immediately visible in your contacts list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The "How We Met" field is particularly valuable for networking. Use it to record details like where you met, mutual connections, conversation topics, or any context that will help you remember and nurture the relationship.
</Note>

## Viewing Contact Details

Click on any contact in your contacts list to view their full profile. The contact detail page includes:

### Profile Card

The main profile card displays:
- Contact's full name with an avatar showing their initial
- **How We Met** context information
- Edit button to update contact information

### Contact Information Panel

The details sidebar shows:
- Email address (with envelope icon)
- Phone number (with phone icon)
- Creator information showing which team member added the contact

### Interaction History

The contact profile includes two tabs for tracking your relationship:

<Accordion title="Events Attended">
Shows all events this contact has been invited to or attended, including:
- Event name and date
- Invitation status (invited or attended)
- Follow-up tasks and their completion status
- Notes from event invitations
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Interaction History">
Displays a chronological log of all logged interactions with this contact, including:
- Date and time of interaction
- User who logged the interaction
- Associated event context
- Detailed notes about the conversation or interaction

This history automatically populates when team members complete follow-up tasks.
</Accordion>

## Editing Contact Information

To update a contact's details:

1. Navigate to the contact's profile page
2. Click the blue edit button (pencil icon) in the top right of the profile card
3. Modify any fields in the contact form
4. Click "Update Contact" to save your changes

## Searching and Filtering Contacts

The contacts list supports powerful search capabilities:

- **Search by name** - Filter contacts by first or last name
- **Search by email** - Find contacts by email address
- **Search by phone** - Filter by phone number
- **Search by context** - Search within the "How We Met" field
- **Filter by events** - Show only contacts who attended specific events

Use the search form at the top of the Contacts page to access these filters.

## Bulk Contact Actions

Trillixe allows you to perform actions on multiple contacts at once:

### Selecting Multiple Contacts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use Checkboxes">
    Click the checkbox next to each contact you want to select, or use the "Select all" checkbox in the table header to select all visible contacts.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Action Bar">
    Once contacts are selected, a floating action bar appears at the bottom of your screen showing how many contacts are selected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bulk Add to Event

To invite multiple contacts to an event:

1. Select the contacts you want to invite using checkboxes
2. In the action bar, choose an event from the "Select Event" dropdown
3. Click the "Add" button
4. The selected contacts will be added as invitations to that event

<Warning>
You can only bulk-add contacts to upcoming events. Past events will not appear in the dropdown.
</Warning>

### Bulk Delete

To remove multiple contacts:

1. Select the contacts you want to delete
2. Click the red "Delete" button in the action bar
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

<Warning>
Deleting a contact is permanent and will also remove all their event invitations, follow-up tasks, and interaction logs. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Contact Ownership

Contacts in Trellixe are shared across your entire team:

- Any team member can add contacts to your account
- All team members can view and edit all contacts
- The contact profile shows which user originally created the contact
- This team-wide visibility enables collaborative relationship management

## Best Practices

<Accordion title="Meaningful Context">
When adding the "How We Met" information, be specific:
- **Good**: "Met at SaaS Growth Conference 2026, discussed scaling challenges, mutual connection: Sarah Chen"
- **Less helpful**: "Conference"

Detailed context helps you and your team reconnect more meaningfully.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Keep Information Current">
Regularly update contact information when you learn new details or when contact information changes. Use the edit function to keep profiles accurate.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Use Event History">
Before reaching out to a contact, review their event history and interaction logs to understand the full context of your relationship and any recent touchpoints.
</Accordion>
