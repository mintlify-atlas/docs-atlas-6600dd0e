## Your Complete Relationship Workflow

Trellixe is built with intention at its heart. It's quick to learn, fast to navigate, and empowering to use:

- **Focused Contact Management** - Store names, emails, phone numbers, and contextual notes
- **One-Tap Attendance Tracking** - Mark who showed up to your events instantly
- **Rich Contextual Notes** - Remember where you met and what you discussed
- **Automated Post-Event Reminders** - Get notified when it's time to follow up
- **Simple Interaction Logging** - Build a complete history of your relationship
- **Clean, Uncluttered Interface** - No bloat, just what you need

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Contacts">
    Create contact profiles with names, contact information, and notes about how you met and what you discussed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Events">
    Set up events like church services, networking meetups, or sales conferences. Invite your contacts to attend.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mark Attendance">
    After the event, mark who actually attended with a single tap. This is the trigger that activates the magic.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Get Reminded">
    Trellixe automatically creates follow-up tasks for every attendee and reminds you at the perfect time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Log Your Interaction">
    After you call or text, log the interaction to build a permanent record of your relationship history.
  </Step>
</Steps>
